Frequently Asked Questions
==========================

## What is Moffstation?
Moffstation is a community-focused MRP server that provides a strong roleplay environment alongside the chaos that defines Space Station.

## How do I join Moffstation?
Moffstation is an invite-only server. To order to join, you must be invited to the Discord by a whitelisted member. Once you’re in the Discord, you’ll need to submit a whitelist application. We typically accept 10–20 applications per week, prioritizing applications that are high-quality or have vouches from existing members.

## Why does Moffstation use a schedule?
A schedule allows us to reach higher player counts while maintaining 100% admin coverage. Check #moffstation-announcements to see the schedule for the current week.

## How do I get a personal item or figurine?
Every member of the server is allowed one personal item and figure per character, with certain restrictions. See [Personal Item Guidelines](personal-items.md) and [Figurine Guidelines](figurines.md) for details. If you aren't sure how to add one, ask around in discussion and someone will likely reach out to help.
